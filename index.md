  - Vibration Plate For Product Testing

    The vibration mount was used by senior engineers to ensure the product passed vibration tests. Due to confidentiality, I cannot show the product, but the mount has threaded holes to attach to the product and unthreaded holes to mount to the testing machine. Furthermore, I modeled the mount and gave the machine shop the 2D drawings for a vibration mount. A mistake with the fabrication arose in the machine shop, and it was up to me to handle the situation.
Note: Standard design for machined products is to polish the surface and have fillets or chamfers. However, this mount was used only by the test engineers, thus polish and fillets are just added costs.

  
    ![VibrationPlate](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/b-smiley/b-smiley.github.io/main/images/VibrationPlate.jpg)
    
  - 3-D Printed Conference Camera Mount

    I was assigned with the design and creation of a camera mount for a Logitech camera that was getting used for conference calls. The problem was that the provided Logitech mount did not work on the bottom of TVs, which is how the company wanted to use it. However, the Logitech mount had good rotational features, so I made a secondary mount to support the Logitech mount. In a future design, I would add cable management to the model. 

    ![CameraMount](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/b-smiley/b-smiley.github.io/main/images/CameraMount.jpg)
